# Do IT Best

Building bridges in IT since 2014.

## Who We Are

Do IT Best is a foundation and community of IT professionals based in Gdańsk, Poland. Since 2014, we've been creating spaces for knowledge sharing, cross-role collaboration, and quality improvement in the IT industry.

We run **3QA** — the Tri-City's quality-focused IT community — and organize the **Improve IT** conference, where developers, testers, analysts, and managers come together to break silos and learn from each other.

## What We Do

### 3QA Community
Monthly meetups bringing together IT professionals to discuss quality, testing, automation, AI, and software delivery. Over 69 events and counting.

### Improve IT Conference
An annual Open Space Technology event where participants set the agenda. No speakers on stage — everyone contributes. Breaking barriers between roles since 2021.

### Knowledge Sharing
We believe the best ideas emerge when diverse perspectives meet. Our events connect people across companies, roles, and experience levels.

## Get Involved

- **Attend a meetup** — Join us at O4 Olivia Business Centre or GPNT in Gdańsk
- **Propose a talk** — Share your experience at 3QA or Improve IT
- **Partner with us** — We collaborate with organizations that share our values

[Learn more about us](about) | [Visit 3QA](https://3qa.pl) | [Improve IT](https://improve-it.org)

# About Do IT Best

## Our Story

Founded in 2015 as a legal entity (KRS 0000553331), Do IT Best Foundation grew out of the 3QA community — a grassroots movement of IT quality enthusiasts in Poland's Trójmiesto region.

What started as informal meetups in 2014 has become a thriving ecosystem of knowledge sharing, professional development, and community building.

## Mission

We promote quality in IT through:

- **Community building** — Creating inclusive spaces where IT professionals connect
- **Knowledge sharing** — Facilitating exchange of ideas, practices, and experiences
- **Cross-role collaboration** — Breaking silos between developers, testers, analysts, and managers
- **Professional development** — Supporting growth at every career stage

# Improve IT — Break the Silos!

An annual Open Space Technology conference where IT professionals come together to break barriers between roles.

## What is Improve IT?

Improve IT is a conference that connects developers, testers, analysts, managers, and anyone who wants to look at projects beyond their own perspective.

**No passive audiences.** In Open Space Technology, participants create the agenda, choose topics, and lead discussions. You decide what we talk about.

## Why Attend?

- **Break silos** — See the same challenges through different roles
- **Get energized** — Open atmosphere encourages sharing and collaboration
- **Get inspired** — Leave with new ideas and contacts
