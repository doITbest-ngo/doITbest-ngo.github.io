## Improve IT Conference

### Overview
- **Type**: Open Space Technology conference
- **Frequency**: Annual (since ~2020, 6 years running)
- **Focus**: Breaking silos between IT roles (devs, testers, analysts, managers)
- **Website**: https://improve-it.org
- **Tagline**: "Break the silos!"
- **Next edition**: 3 October 2026 (Saturday), Gdańsk

### Editions
- **2021** — First edition, traditional conference format
- **2023** — Switched to Open Space Technology
- **2024** — "Break the silos! vol.2", Open Space
- **2025** — Open Space (upcoming/planned)
- **2026** — 3 October, Open Space

### Partners
- Hapag-Lloyd Knowledge Center Gdańsk
- Agile 3M
- Stowarzyszenie Jakości Systemów Informatycznych (SJSI — Polish Testing Board)

### What is Open Space Technology?
- No predefined agenda — participants create it live
- Anyone can propose a topic and lead a discussion
- Encourages cross-role collaboration and organic knowledge sharing
- Can scale to thousands of participants

## Mission & Values

2. **Knowledge sharing** — creating space for learning and exchange
3. **Community building** — connecting IT professionals across roles and companies
4. **Breaking silos** — fostering cross-discipline collaboration
5. **Inclusivity** — open to all roles in IT (devs, testers, analysts, managers, enthusiasts)
