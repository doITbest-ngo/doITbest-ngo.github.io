We collaborate with organizations that share our commitment to quality in IT.

## SJSI — Polish Testing Board

Stowarzyszenie Jakości Systemów Informatycznych (SJSI) is a non-profit supporting IT community development through certification, community activation, and knowledge sharing.

**Website**: [sjsi.org](https://sjsi.org)

## Agile 3M

An informal group focused on exchanging knowledge and experiences about agile methods and practices — Scrum, Kanban, Lean, XP. Co-organizers of Improve IT.

## Hapag-Lloyd Knowledge Center Gdańsk

Technology center of one of the world's largest shipping companies, based in Gdańsk. Partners and sponsors of Improve IT.

**Website**: [knowledgecenter.pl](https://knowledgecenter.pl)
